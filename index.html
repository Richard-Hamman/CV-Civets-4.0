A career portal is a website on which companies list their available vacancies. Most companies are aware of, and often use the industry-leading career portal to find candidates.

These career portals include P-net, Career Junction, Indeed, Grapevine, Careers24, Glassdoor, Career Jet, BestJobs, Bizcommunity, Compujobs, Hoteljobs and more.

An ATS is an Applicant Tracking System. They are used by many large companies and recruiters to manage applicants that respond to their job ads. These systems use algorithms to scan CVs and then decide whether an applicant qualifies for the position using specific keywords.

POPIA Compliance Notice (South Africa)

Effective Date: 20 January, 2020

CV Civets (Pty) Ltd respects your privacy and is committed to processing your personal information lawfully and transparently in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information Collected We collect and process personal information such as: Your name, email address, and contact details CV, LinkedIn, and job application information you provide Website usage data (via cookies and analytics)

2. Purpose of Collection Your information is collected and used to: Deliver requested CV writing and related services Communicate with you about your services and account Maintain accurate business records Improve service quality and website functionality Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing of Personal Information We do not share your personal information without your consent except: Where required by law With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality

4. Your Rights Under POPIA, you have the right to: Access and request correction of your personal information Object to processing under certain circumstances Request deletion of your information where legally permissible For all data-related requests, please email: orders@cvcivets.co.za.

5. Security Measures We implement reasonable security safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

6. Complaints If you believe your rights have been infringed, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa): Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za

Available Job Portals in South Africa

Where can I find work in South Africa?

Are you looking for employment in your desired field?

Here is a comprehensive list of popular and speciality job portals to help you find the perfect job in your field.

First, let's start with the popular job portals:

LinkedIn

Offers job vacancies from a variety of sources and allows users to network with professionals in their field.

CareerJunction

One of the largest job portals in South Africa with a wide range of job vacancies.

PNet

Offers a large number of job vacancies and allows users to upload their CV for recruiters to find.

Indeed

Offers job vacancies from a variety of sources, including company websites and job boards.

Gumtree

Offers a large number of job vacancies, including part-time and freelance jobs.

Now, let's take a look at the speciality job portals:

Jobs.co.za

Focuses on IT and engineering job vacancies.

Jobmail

Offers job vacancies in the construction, engineering, and mining industries.

Bizcommunity

Offers job vacancies in the marketing, advertising, and media industries.

Jobvine

Offers job vacancies in various industries, including IT, sales, and marketing.

Topcareer

TopCareer: Focuses on job vacancies in the finance, accounting, and legal industries.

These job portals can be a useful tool in your job search.

By utilizing both popular and speciality job portals, you increase your chances of finding the perfect job.

However, don't forget to also be proactive in your job search by reaching out to potential employers directly.

Goodluck !
